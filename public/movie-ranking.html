The Best Movies For a Bad Day According to Jessica

My top 3 comfort movies

Tangled

This movie is fun and lighthearded with great songs to sing along to. It brings me joy whenever I watch it.

The Greatest Showman

Who can be sad when they are listening to Hugh Jackman sing. An added bonus is the lighthearted theme of finding your place in a world that doesn't want to accept you.

How to Train Your Dragon

I don't care if it is a kid's movie, dragons are freaking cool. Also who doesn't love Toothless and his adorable nature.
